ABOUT DANIEL

Daniel has been a musician all his life, and developed an exceptionally trained ear for rhythm and and harmony. This means that his DJ sets are very precise, with seamless transitions between multiple genres and a constant flow of rhythm as he takes his audience on a journey that he has tailored specifically for them.

 

Whether it's a diverse wedding group, a private party or even a corporate event, you can count on Daniel to deliver a memorable set that your attendees will love.

 

Daniel would love to speak with you today about your DJ needs - if you'd like to work out a pre-planned set or leave him to read-the-room, he will take your event to the next level.
